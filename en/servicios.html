Amenidades

Everything your vessel and crew need, without leaving the Palmira complex.

⚓

Dock Slips · Wet Marina

42 slips with power and water for vessels up to 120 feet. 110v and 220v electrical supply, potable water at every slip, and dock use for visiting vessels.

From $22 USD/ft per month

🛥️

Marina Seca

On-land storage for boats up to 39 feet. Includes launch ramp use and lowboy maneuvering for vessels up to 45 feet.

From $2,290 MXN per month

🏗️

Hangar Rentals

Covered spaces offering full protection from sun, rain and salt air. The best option for long stays or vessels requiring premium preservation.

$10,780 MXN per month

📡

Navigation Support

Port Captain weather reports, radio navigation assistance and maritime signaling. Everything you need to depart and arrive safely.

🔒

24-Hour Security

24-hour security personnel and 24-hour CCTV camera system. Plus on-site fire-fighting equipment and a hazardous waste room for responsible disposal.

🚿

Crew Services

Restrooms and showers, laundry, on-site WiFi and trash collection. Full comfort for crews on short or long stays.

🛒

Marina Market

Marine goods and accessories for sale within the complex. Parts, safety equipment and basic supplies steps from the dock.

⛽

Supplies & Utilities

Marine diesel, potable water ($7.50 USD/m³) and 110v/220v electricity ($0.53 USD/kw) metered by actual use. Invoicing available.

📋

Paperwork & Clearance

Assistance with Topolobampo Port Captain clearance, departure processing for Mexican and international vessels, TIP guidance and sport fishing permits.
